Dodgeball!

Blue is controlled using the WASD keys and shoots using Caps Lock. Red is controlled using the 4 arrow keys and shoots with the Right Enter Key. Space resets the round after someone wins. To pick up a ball just hover over it and then shoot at your opponent. You get a point when your opponent is hit. The balls start out slow and progressively get faster.
